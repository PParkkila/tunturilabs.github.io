Hobbymax Platform

Tunturi Labs builds Hobbymax, a flexible hobby tracking system for reading, learning, and any custom personal tracking workflows. Designed to adapt to how you actually organize your life, not the other way around.

Data Ownership First

Your data belongs to you. Hobbymax is built around strong data ownership principles, ensuring you can export, control, and retain full access to your information at any time.

Privacy by Design

We minimize data collection and avoid unnecessary tracking. Hobbymax is engineered to keep personal activity private, secure, and isolated from third-party access.
