HobbyMax

A personal hobby tracking platform for reading, learning, and breaking free from social media addiction.

Privacy by Design

HobbyMax is built with privacy as a core principle. Your activity data is not used for profiling, advertising, or third-party analytics. What you track stays under your control.

Full Data Ownership

Your data belongs to you, always. Export your information at any time, use it however you want, and never be locked into the platform.

Flexible Hobby Tracking

Track reading progress, habits, goals, or completely custom hobbies. HobbyMax is designed to support structured and unstructured tracking equally well.

Early Access / Beta

HobbyMax is actively evolving. If you're interested in early access or testing, contact us at beta@tunturilabs.com.
